What are the benefits of implementing cyber security in your company?

EndPoint Monitoring

What is EndPoint Monitoring? Our EndPoint services install proprietary software onto system infrastructure that acts as a IDS (Intrustion Detection System) and IPS (Intrustion Prevention System). Our EndPoint Monitoring allows our software to work on the front lines of your companies cyber secruity infrastructure.

Web Application Security & Protection

Web Application Security refers to the protection and security of website code. We protect your companies valuable website infrastructure by implementing vulnerability patching and protection for threats such as cross-site-scripting (XSS) and SQL injection methods.

Threat Response and Escalation

Threat Response and Escalation is our process of indentifying, stopping, and tracing a possible threat actor. This is the prime service in Cyber Security that ensures a safe recovery and plan of action during a data breach.

Full Network Protection

Full Network Protection is our top-of-the-line service that allows coverage of network infrastructure. Full Network Protection ensures that unauthroized and suspicious access is monitored and logged through our software. Ensuring a fully protected network that is safe from hidden or unwanted visitors.
